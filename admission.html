Admission Requirements | S.Y. 2020-2021

A student seeking admission to this University must meet the following minimum requirements:

For Senior High School (K-12):

a. NCAE Result

b. PSA/NSO Birth Certificate

c. 1x1 & 2x2 Picture (White Background)

d. Long Brown & Plastic Envelope (2)

For Incoming College Students:

a. NSO/PSA Certified Birth Certificate (Original & 2 Photocopy)

b. High School/Grade 12 Card (Original & 1 Photocopy)

c. Form 137-a (If already available)(Original & 1 Photocopy)

d. Certified of Good Moral Character (Original)

e. NCAE Result (if any:)

f. 4pcs 2x2 Picture (Formal; White Background)

g. 4pcs 1x1 Picture (Formal; White Background)

h. Medical Certificate (Optional)

FOR TRANSFEREES:

a. NSO/PSA Certified Birth Certificate (Original & 2 Photocopy)

b. Transfer Credential with Certificates of Good Moral and Grades

c. Transcript of Records (TOR) - Copy for Asian Institute of Technology and Education

d. NCAE Result (if any:)

e. 4pcs 2x2 Picture (Formal; White Background)

f. 4pcs 1x1 Picture (Formal; White Background)

g. Medical Certificate (Optional)

Applicants (For New Freshmen and Student Transferees) must pass the entrance examination and interview.
